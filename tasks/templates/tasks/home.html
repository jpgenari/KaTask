How to Use

View All Tasks at Once in a Single View

Under Tasks menu, view all tasks, one by one piled up, with what needs to be done first - ordered by uncompleted, due date, priority and older tasks first.

Create, Edit, Complete and Delete Tasks

Create your tasks by clicking at 'New Task', edit existing tasks by clicking at any available details - from task title to priority and don't worry if you don't find a Category yet -, Complete and Delete tasks.

View All Tasks Assigned to Your Categories

Under Categories menu, create and view all categories and tasks assigned to them. Click on a Category to view assigned tasks, Edit and Delete categories.
